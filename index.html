A Century of Inflation and Economic Uncertainty: Putting the COVID-19 Shock in Context

Fatema Tuj Johra, Victor J. Valcarcel

This paper examines how COVID 19 induced macro volatility compared with earlier disruptive episodes in historical macroeconomic experience. Using a time-varying parameter VAR (TVP-VAR), the paper examines macroeconomic volatility in real GDP growth and inflation.
